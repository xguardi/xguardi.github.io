Datacamp project with Candy Crush data
Jul 10, 2018

A bit of shameless self-promotion here.

Recently, I’ve published a project (mini-course) on the Datacamp platform. Here’s the link to the project:

Level difficulty in Candy Crush Saga

The course lets you hone your R skills by playing with actual Candy Crush data. By following the exercice you’ll learn how to compute and visualize the levels difficulty and understand how level designers make use of such insights.

Datacamp is a great learning platform and the people behind it are very talented. I do use it from time to time to refresh my Python/R skills.
